## 4. Topology Semantic Decomposition

### 4.1 Primary Topology Namespaces

| Namespace | Purpose | Domain | Authority |
|-----------|---------|--------|-----------|
| `morphology_topology` | Spatial structure relationships | MRP | Governance-scoped |
| `surface_topology` | Geometric surface continuity | CAM | Manufacturing-scoped |
| `manufacturing_topology` | Shell construction for CAM | CAM | Operational-scoped |

### 4.2 Why Three Namespaces, Not Two

```
morphology_topology ≠ surface_topology ≠ manufacturing_topology
```

| Concept | Meaning | Evidence |
|---------|---------|----------|
| morphology_topology | Spatial relationships, regions, connectivity | `canonical_ontology_registry.py:472` |
| surface_topology | G0/G1/G2 continuity at geometric junctions | `topology_builder/contracts.py:16` |
| manufacturing_topology | 3D shell construction for CAM execution | `topology_builder/contracts.py:25` |

These are **related but NOT equivalent**.

Collapsing them would:
- Merge governance semantics with manufacturing semantics
- Conflate spatial relationships with geometric continuity
- Create false equivalence between MRP and CAM domains
