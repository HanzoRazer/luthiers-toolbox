## Notes

1. Compensation values are reference starting points; calibrate per instrument during setup
2. All internal calculations use mm; inches provided for reference
3. Scale/intonation math handled by `instrument_geometry.neck.fret_math` and `bridge.compensation`
4. 17-inch lower bout width is characteristic of Benedetto La Venezia and similar models
5. Top and back are hand-graduated carved arches; CNC rough carving possible, final graduation by hand
6. Floating bridge and tailpiece — no bridge pin holes or saddle slot routing needed
7. Floating pickup on pickguard bracket — no pickup routing in body
