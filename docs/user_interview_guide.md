## Part 1: Background & Current Workflow (8 minutes)

### 1. Tell me about your guitar building experience.
- _Probe:_ How long have you been building? Pro or hobbyist?
- _Probe:_ What types of guitars do you make? (acoustic, electric, archtop?)
- _Listen for:_ Skill level, scale of operation, specialization

### 2. Walk me through your design process, from idea to CNC-ready files.
- _Probe:_ What tools do you use? (Fusion 360, VCarve, SketchUp, paper?)
- _Probe:_ Where do you spend the most time?
- _Listen for:_ Pain points, tool switching, manual calculations

### 3. What's the most frustrating part of your current workflow?
- _Probe:_ What makes you want to throw your computer out the window?
- _Probe:_ Have you ever abandoned a design because it was too tedious?
- _Listen for:_ Specific pain points we can solve

### 4. How do you handle specialized calculations? (fretboard radius, rosette dimensions, string spacing, etc.)
- _Probe:_ Do you use spreadsheets, online calculators, or do it manually?
- _Probe:_ How much time does this add to your workflow?
- _Listen for:_ Need for integrated calculators

### 5. Tell me about your DXF export process.
- _Probe:_ Do your files import cleanly into CAM software?
- _Probe:_ How often do you have to fix DXF files manually?
- _Listen for:_ CAM compatibility issues

## Part 2: Tool Box Demo & Feedback (12 minutes)

> "Now I'd like to show you our tool and get your honest feedback. Remember, this is a prototype‚Äîthere will be rough edges."

**Share screen:** Show Luthier's Tool Box interface

### 6. First impressions: What do you notice first?
- _Listen for:_ UI confusion, visual appeal, what draws attention

### 7. Let me show you the rosette designer. [Demo feature]
   What do you think?
- _Probe:_ Would you use this? Why or why not?
- _Probe:_ What's missing or confusing?

### 8. Here's the curve math tool. [Demo offset, fillet, fair]
   Would this be helpful in your work?
- _Probe:_ What would you use this for?
- _Probe:_ How does this compare to your current method?
- _Listen for:_ Real use cases, comparison to existing tools

### 9. And here's the DXF export functionality. [Demo export]
   Does this format work for your CAM software?
- _Probe:_ What CAM software do you use? (Fusion 360, VCarve, Mach4?)
- _Probe:_ What issues do you typically encounter with DXF imports?
- _Listen for:_ CAM compatibility requirements

### 10. Which feature would you use most? Which is least useful?
- _Probe:_ Rank top 3 features
- _Listen for:_ Feature priorities

### 11. What's missing? If you could add one feature, what would it be?
- _Listen for:_ Feature gaps, unmet needs

---

## Part 3: Pricing & Adoption (5 minutes)

### 12. If this tool saved you [X hours per week], what would you pay for it?
- _Probe:_ Monthly subscription? One-time purchase? Free with premium tier?
- _Probe:_ What price point would be "too expensive"?
- _Probe:_ What price would be "too cheap to trust"?
- _Listen for:_ Willingness to pay, pricing expectations

### 13. Would you switch from your current tools to this?
- _Probe:_ What would it take for you to switch?
- _Probe:_ What concerns do you have?
- _Listen for:_ Switching barriers, must-have features

### 14. Who else in the lutherie community would benefit from this?
- _Probe:_ Lutherie schools? CNC shops? Hobbyists?
- _Probe:_ Would you recommend this to colleagues?
- _Listen for:_ Target market insights, referral potential

## Tips for Great Interviews

‚úÖ **DO:**
- Ask open-ended questions ("Tell me about..." not "Do you like...?")
- Listen 80%, talk 20%
- Probe deeper: "Why?" "Can you tell me more?" "Show me an example?"
- Take notes on tone/emotion, not just words
- Stay neutral‚Äîdon't defend your product

‚ùå **DON'T:**
- Lead the witness: "You'd love this feature, right?"
- Interrupt or finish their sentences
- Explain too much‚Äîlet them explore the tool
- Argue or get defensive about criticism
- Promise features you can't deliver
