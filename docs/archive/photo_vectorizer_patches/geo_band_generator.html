Marquetry Notes
Twisted rope inlay technique:
• Cut each strand from 0.6mm veneer
• Strands interleave physically—
  one end tucked under next
• Pocket routed in one pass
• Strand order: back strand first,
  then overlay forward strands
• CA glue, scrape flush, buff
