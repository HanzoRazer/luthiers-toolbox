  ---
  Series Variants

  Production (Primary)

  - Headless design
  - Steinberger-style tuner housing
  - Locking nut + fine tuners at bridge
  - Shorter overall length: 812.8mm

  Pro

  - Traditional Flying V-style headstock
  - 6-inline locking tuners
  - ~190mm longer than Production
  - Optional floating tremolo or Floyd Rose tremolo

  Design Lineage

  - Primary parent: Gibson Explorer — body shape, neck geometry, scale length
  - Secondary: Gibson Les Paul — set-neck construction philosophy
  - Body shape: Original ergonomic design by Ross L. Echols Jr. — asymmetric ergonomic voids for arm/ribcage relief
