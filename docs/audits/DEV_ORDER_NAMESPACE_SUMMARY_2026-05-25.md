## Namespace Conventions

### Prefixes
- `MRP-` — Morphology Reconstruction Platform
- `CAM-` — CAM/Export Pipeline
- `IBG-` — Instrument Body Geometry
- `DO` — Numeric dev orders (cross-cutting)
- `Phase` — Implementation phases within a track

### Numbering
- Series (1-9): Major domain area
- Letter (A-Z): Sequential within series
- Compound (QR, etc.): Merged orders

### Status Values
- **Complete** — Delivered and merged
- **BLOCKED** — Awaiting external dependency
- **Imported** — Brought in from another repo
