The Process

What Happens Next

We keep things personal and unhurried. Here's what to expect after you send your inquiry.

1

We Review & Reach Out

Every inquiry is read personally by James Hartwell. We'll review your specifications and vision, then reply by email within 3–5 business days to introduce ourselves and answer any initial questions.

Within 3–5 business days
2

Consultation Call

We'll schedule a 30–45 minute call (video or phone) to talk through your instrument in depth — tonewood options, body style, playability needs, ornamentation, and timeline. This call is free and carries no obligation.

30–45 min, no commitment
3

Proposal & Deposit

After the call, we'll send a detailed written proposal with finalized specifications and pricing. If you'd like to proceed, a 25% deposit secures your place in the build queue and the journey begins.

Queue position confirmed
