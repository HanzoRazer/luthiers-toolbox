# Luthier's Toolbox - Guitar Plans Archive Reference

## Executive Summary

This document catalogs **200+ instrument plan files** discovered across the archived lutherie project folders. The archives contain professional-grade blueprints, DXF/DWG CAD files, and PDF plans spanning:

- **Electric Guitars**: 50+ models (Fender, Gibson, PRS, Ibanez, etc.)
- **Acoustic Guitars**: 20+ models (Dreadnought, OM, Jumbo, Classical, etc.)
- **Archtop/Jazz**: 10+ models (ES-335, Benedetto, D'Aquisto, etc.)
- **Bass Guitars**: 5+ models (Jazz Bass, Acoustic Bass, Electric Bass)
- **Other Instruments**: Mandolin, Octave Mandolin, Ukulele, Bouzouki, Lute
- **Mottola Custom Designs**: 14 original acoustic and electric designs
- **Reference Materials**: Archtop measurement kits, graduation maps, construction guides

### Current Catalog Status
The Luthier's Toolbox application currently has **21 instruments** in its catalog with DXF body outlines ready for CNC. Many more can be extracted from these archives using AI vision processing.
