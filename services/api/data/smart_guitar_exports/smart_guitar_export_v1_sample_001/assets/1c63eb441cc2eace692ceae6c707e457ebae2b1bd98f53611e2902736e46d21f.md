# The Major Scale Pattern

## Overview
The major scale is the foundation of Western music theory.

## Pattern (Key of G)

e|------------------2--3--|
B|--------------0--3------|
G|----------0--2----------|
D|------0--2--------------|
A|--0--2------------------|
E|--3---------------------|

## Intervals
W - W - H - W - W - W - H

(W = Whole step, H = Half step)

## Practice Tips

1. Say note names as you play
2. Practice ascending and descending
3. Use a metronome (start at 60 BPM)
4. Visualize the pattern shape

## Application
- Improvisation foundation
- Understanding chord construction
- Ear training reference
