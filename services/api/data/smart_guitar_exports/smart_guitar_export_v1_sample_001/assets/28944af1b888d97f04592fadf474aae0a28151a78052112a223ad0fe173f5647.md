# Five Minute Daily Warmup

## Purpose
A consistent warmup routine prevents injury and improves technique over time.

## Routine (5 minutes total)

### Minute 1: Finger Stretches
- Spread fingers wide, hold 10 seconds
- Make a fist, hold 5 seconds
- Repeat 3 times

### Minutes 2-3: Chromatic Exercise
Play frets 1-2-3-4 on each string:
- Use one finger per fret
- Alternate picking
- 60-80 BPM

### Minutes 4-5: Chord Changes
Practice switching between:
- G to C (4 times)
- C to D (4 times)
- D to G (4 times)

## Tips
- Never skip warmup
- Increase tempo gradually over weeks
- Listen to your body - stop if pain occurs
